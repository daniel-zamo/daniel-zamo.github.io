## Architectural Vision

This section documents the **modernization of middleware platforms**. The goal is to transition monolithic, manual infrastructures toward elastic, code-managed architectures.

### The Business Problem

In regulated sectors (Banking, Insurance, Telco), traditional management of application servers (JBoss, WildFly, WebSphere) introduces critical friction:

*   **Operational Risk:** Manual configuration leads to human error and Environment Drift.
*   **Maintenance Cost:** Excessive time spent on manual patching and updates (Toil) hinders innovation.
*   **Slow Recovery:** High RTOs (Recovery Time Objectives) due to the lack of automated provisioning.

**Implemented Solution:** Immutable Infrastructure + Orchestration.
